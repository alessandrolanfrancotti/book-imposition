Lil Imposer is my small contribution to the DIY fanzine world.
Me and my friends were always wasting tons of time doing stupid wrong impositions by hand, or trying to use some insane, overcomplicated software.

We just wanted to print, fold some paper and end up with a book.
At some point I had to step in, for the good of the community.

So I made this website: a simple online tool, no installs, no bullshit, to do imposition straight from your browser.

It’s made for fanzines, print tests, ugly books, nice books, broken books.
For designers, artists, students, anyone who wants to print stuff without losing their mind.

Swag designers, have fun.
Print. Fold. Repeat.

HOW TO EXPORT PROPERLY

> HOW TO EXPORT PROPERLY

> BLEED:
> 3 mm on outer, top and bottom.
> No bleed on the inner (binding) side.
> Inner bleed won’t kill anyone, but avoid it
> to prevent weird lines and artifacts in the inner margin.

> MARKS:
> ONLY crop marks.
> No registration marks.
> No page info, no color bars, no print info,
> no extra crap.
> Extra marks will confuse the tool and break the imposition.

> NO bullshit. ONLY crop marks.
